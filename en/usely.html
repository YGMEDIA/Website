The Idea

Accounting that does not
feel like accounting.

The path from quote to invoice eats time and keeps you from the actual work. That is exactly what USELY is built for: you stand at the customer, type in the items, see the price instantly and save everything as a project. If the customer says yes, one tap turns it into the quote, the order confirmation and finally the invoice. All legally linked, all in your pocket, all in seconds instead of hours.

Features

Everything in.
Nothing in the way.

From the first calculation to the EÜR export for your tax advisor. Every feature is built to a single standard: simple and fast.

Calculation

Calculated in 60 seconds.

Type in the items, see the price instantly, save it as a project. Right on site at the customer, as simple as a calculator. No laptop, no Excel, no evening at the desk.

  • Totals update in real time while you type
  • VAT applied automatically based on your company default
  • Every calculation becomes a quote with one tap
Document chain

From quote to invoice.

Quote, order confirmation, partial invoice and final invoice in one seamless document chain. Every step legally linked, every number in a clean numbering sequence.

  • An accepted quote becomes an order confirmation with one tap
  • Create partial invoices directly from the project
  • The final invoice automatically deducts paid partial invoices
Projects & customers

One project holds it all together.

Calculation, documents and expenses live in one place, assigned to each customer. You see at a glance what is open, what has been paid and where a job currently stands.

  • Customer management with automatic customer numbers
  • Address autocompletion when creating entries
  • Status of every document at a glance: open, sent, paid
Expenses & receipts

Scan instead of typing.

Photograph a receipt, the text is recognized automatically, amount and category are captured instantly. Expenses can be assigned directly to a project, including recurring fixed costs.

  • Automatic text recognition right on the iPhone
  • Recurring expenses are created automatically
  • Every expense optionally assigned to a project
Legal compliance

Legally compliant from day one.

You do not have to know tax law, USELY knows it for you. All mandatory details and regulations are handled automatically, on every single document.

  • GoBD-conform: immutable, traceable documents
  • All mandatory details under §14 UStG handled automatically
  • Small business regulation under §19 UStG supported
  • GDPR: data export and deletion at any time
E-invoicing & export

Ready for your tax advisor.

XRechnung and ZUGFeRD for the e-invoicing mandate, plus the EÜR export as PDF and CSV. At the end of the year you export everything with a few taps, cleanly prepared and complete.

  • E-invoices as XRechnung and ZUGFeRD
  • Income statement (EÜR) as PDF and CSV
  • Complete data export of your accounting

The Story

From the construction site
to the App Store.

The idea for USELY was born in 2022 in real working life: observing on the construction site and talking to business owners in the personal environment. The path from quote to invoice was the same painful topic everywhere. It costs evenings, is annoying and keeps people from the actual work.

That observation became a first web version, which regional press already reported on back then. Supported by IGZ Würzburg, Brainstation Lauda and Campus Founders, the prototype grew into a well-thought-out product: today a completely newly built, native iOS app, live in the App Store.

USELY is entirely built solo: concept, branding, UI/UX design, iOS development, backend and launch from one source by YG MEDIA from Würzburg, Germany.
