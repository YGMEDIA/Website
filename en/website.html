The price depends on the scope. A simple business website starts at 1,500 euros. More complex projects with SEO, multiple languages or online shop integration can cost more. An honest overview of all price ranges is available in the cost guide. In our intro call you get a concrete quote with no hidden costs.
A standard business website is finished in 2 to 4 weeks. More complex projects take 4 to 8 weeks. The exact timeline is defined in the intro call and I stick to it.
With all CMS and builder systems like WordPress, Framer, Shopify or Wix you get an onboarding after the launch and can update texts, images and content yourself. With fully custom-built HTML websites, self-maintenance is technically more demanding. In that case we take care of ongoing maintenance and updates for you.
That depends on your goal, your budget and your requirements. A short overview:

Framer: ideal for modern landing pages, portfolios and startups that want to launch quickly with a high-quality, animated presence. Great performance, easy to use.

WordPress: the classic for larger websites with a blog, many pages or more complex structures. Huge plugin ecosystem, very flexible and easy to maintain yourself.

Shopify: the first choice for e-commerce. If you want to sell products online, Shopify is the strongest option. Integrated payments, inventory management and shop features included.

Wix: fine for simple websites you want to maintain yourself. Quick to set up, but limited in flexibility and design quality.

Custom HTML development: the strongest solution for maximum individuality. No third-party dependencies, no plugin updates, no limitations. Everything is built exactly the way it is needed. Recommended when uniqueness, scalability and full control are the priority.
I help you choose and set up hosting and domain. You keep full control and pay the provider directly. I recommend reliable, GDPR-compliant providers in Germany.
