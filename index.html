FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you understand my business or just design?+
Both. Every project starts with a strategic brief. We research your market, competitors, and target audience before touching a pixel or writing a single ad.
Do I need to bring technical know-how?+
No. We handle the technical side. You focus on your business. We communicate clearly at every step so you always know what is happening.
What does a project cost?+
Projects start at €1,000 for focused deliverables and scale based on scope. We offer fixed-price projects and monthly retainers. Book a free call to get a clear quote within 48 hours.
Do you work with templates?+
Never. Every project is built from scratch, specific to your brand, your audience, and your goals.
Design and development — both with you?+
Yes. We design and build. No hand-off to a separate developer. One point of contact, full ownership, from concept to live.
Do you work with startups in Limassol or Cyprus?+
Yes, actively. YG MEDIA is based in Limassol and working with local and international startups in tech, fintech, and gaming. Reach out directly.
Can you help us enter the German market?+
Yes. DACH market strategy is a core specialization. We run German Google Ads, optimize UX for German-speaking users, and position brands for Germany, Austria, and Switzerland.
